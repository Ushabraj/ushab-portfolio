

# Portfolio Website for Usha B — AI & ML Engineer

## Design Foundation
- **Theme**: Dark mode with deep charcoal/black background and teal/blue tech accent colors
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts (Inter or similar) with strong heading contrast
- **Layout**: Card-based, generous spacing, minimalistic and uncluttered
- **Effects**: Subtle glow effects, smooth scroll, hover animations, neural network-inspired background patterns
- **Fully responsive** for mobile, tablet, and desktop
